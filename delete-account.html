⚠ Account Deletion

Delete Your UpWella Account

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time. Please read the information below before submitting your request.

📋 How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to admin@upwella.health from the email address linked to your UpWella account.
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your full name and the email address associated with your account.
  4. We will confirm receipt within 2 business days and complete the deletion within 30 days.

🗂 What data is deleted or retained

Data type Action Retention period
Profile information (name, weight, goals) Deleted Immediately upon deletion
Food diary & meal plans Deleted Immediately upon deletion
Progress photos & measurements Deleted Immediately upon deletion
Authentication credentials (Firebase) Deleted Immediately upon deletion
Subscription & billing records Retained 7 years (legal / tax obligations)

Ready to submit your request?

Email us directly and we'll take care of everything. No forms, no fuss.

✉ Email admin@upwella.health

Note: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. If you have an active subscription, please cancel it before requesting deletion to avoid future charges. Deletion does not automatically cancel a Google Play or App Store subscription.
